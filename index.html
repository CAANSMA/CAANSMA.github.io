Chinese-American Assocation of Northboro and Southboro

Our Mission

The Chinese-American Association for Northborough and Southborough aims to promote Chinese culture and heritage in the Northborough and Southborough communities. As a community-centered nonprofit organization, we celebrate Chinese cultural activities, holidays, and festivities as well as engage in cultural conversations to form bridges between the Chinese-American community and the Northborough-Southborough communities.


Northborough 和 Southborough华裔美国人协会旨在促进 Northborough 和 Southborough 区的中国文化和遗产。作为一个以社区为中心的非营利组织，我们庆祝中国文化活动、节日和庆祝活动，并参与文化对话，以在华裔美国人社区和 Northborough-Southborough 社区之间架起桥梁。
